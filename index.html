About Me:

Hello! My name is Edgardo and I am self taught programmer. My journey began in March of 2022 after meeting up with a good friend from college who inspired me to make a career change. But before I get to that, first let me tell you a bit about who I am. I was born and raised in Los Angeles. I am the youngest sibling of four. I am a first generation Mexican-American and I enjoy sports, movies, and spending time with my family. After high school I attened Brown University where I studied economics. Upon graduation I returned home to Los Angeles where I worked in food and beverage at a company called Levy Restaurants as an Accounts Payable Clerk. After 2 years at Levy, I went on to work at a Property Management company where I was a supervisor for the maintenance department.
After 4 long years, I finally decided that I needed to make a change: I was going to teach myself to program! Shout out to my boy Mario Vega for putting me up on game when it comes to programming and making me realize that I can too can learn to code if I put in the time and effort. With a mentor in my corner and my determination to get away from a paycheck to paycheck lifestyle, I decided to bet on myself and embark on my long journey of becoming a programmer.
Now I have to admit, it has been harder and taken longer than I expected but I'm still at it. It's comforting to know that learning to code is very similar to working out: It's a life long journer, it's truly never ending. There is always something new to learn. Knowing that, helps me in times of frustration when I feel like I'm learning to slow or don't understand a topic. But so far, I've gotten approximately 30% of the way through the Full Stack Course on Codecademy and my current project is to build my personal website. So here it is! What do you think??
Feel free to leave me a comment on how I can improve this site!
I hope to continue learning front-end developement skills and hope to persue an entry level job in wed design within in the next 6-8 months.
